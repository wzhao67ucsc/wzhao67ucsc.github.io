The Challenge

The job search process is often slow and repetitive. The goal was to build a system that uses AI to bridge the gap between a user’s experience and a recruiter's expectations, making it easier to land interviews.

Product Features

AI Resume Optimization

The system automatically rewrites bullet points to match the specific job description a user is targeting.

Automated Job Scraping

Users can save jobs from any URL directly to their dashboard. The tool extracts all job data automatically.

Full Platform Overhaul

Redesigned every major part of the site, including the hero section, landing page, payments, and login systems.

UX/UI Iteration

Refined the interface based on usage data from over 30,000 people across various global markets.
